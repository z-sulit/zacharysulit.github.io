About Myself

I am Zachary Lorenzo F. Sulit, in the first year of BS in Data Science at Ateneo de Davao University. I don't consider myself talented or knowledgeable in coding; I am pretty bad at it. This also includes my mathematical skills. I have not picked any other major that is different from computer studies because I don't find them fun. Medicine, Accountancy, or Engineering does not inspire me to improve or study any of its subjects. But when it comes to Computer Studies, I find it interesting. I get to learn code and the history of how our world constantly relies on it. However, why Data Science? I chose this course because even though my mathematical skills are sub-par, I like to solve math as I find it satisfying when I solve a problem. The same applies to programming. Seeing the things I work on gives me pleasure and makes me happy. I also chose Data science because it is something new, and I want to be the first to explore it. Analyzing data is fascinating, and I appreciate the value it can bring to society.
